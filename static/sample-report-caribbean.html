Tax & legal flags

Occupancy tax: The DR charges an 18% ITBIS (VAT) on rental income. Platforms may or may not collect — verify with a local accountant.

Property tax: IPI (property tax) is 1% on value above ~RD$9.9M (~$170K). CONFOTUR-certified properties are exempt for 15 years.

Income tax: DR taxes rental income at 27%. US owners must also report on Schedule E and may claim Foreign Tax Credits. File FBAR/FATCA if aggregate foreign accounts exceed $10K.

Depreciation: Building (~85% of $208,577 = $177,290) / 27.5 yrs = ~$6,447/yr for US tax purposes (40-year schedule may apply to foreign property — consult CPA).

14-day rule: US 14-day rule still applies to foreign properties. Personal use >14 days or >10% of rental days may limit deductions.

Mortgage interest: Mortgage interest on foreign property is deductible against rental income on Schedule E.
