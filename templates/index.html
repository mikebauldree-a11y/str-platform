Common questions

How is this different from running my own spreadsheet?
The report uses market-specific revenue benchmarks, seasonal ADR curves, and expense models built for each market — data that takes hours to assemble yourself. For markets like Aruba or the Dominican Republic, it also flags country-specific tax and regulatory issues most investors miss entirely.
My property isn't in one of your listed markets. Can I still get a report?
Yes. Select the closest market type (Coastal Premium, Coastal Standard, etc.) and the report will use appropriate benchmarks. You can also enter a custom gross revenue figure if you have local comps.
What does "interactive HTML" mean?
Along with a PDF, you'll get an HTML version of the report that renders charts, scenario tables, and risk grids visually — easier to read and share than a flat document.
Is this financial advice?
No — it's an analytical tool. The report surfaces the numbers and flags the risks; the investment decision is yours. Think of it as a thorough due-diligence worksheet.
What markets do you cover?
Caribbean: Aruba, Dominican Republic, Bahamas, Turks & Caicos, Jamaica, Cayman Islands, Barbados, Puerto Rico, USVI. US Coastal: Outer Banks NC, Carolina Beach NC, Myrtle Beach SC, Destin FL, 30A FL. Additional markets available on request.
